1. Getting Started

When you first open the application, you will be greeted by the landing page. Click "Get Started" to begin your new project.

2. Uploading Your Media

The upload screen allows you to drag and drop your file or select it from your computer.

  • Supported Formats: MP3 (Audio) and MP4 (Video).
  • Size Limit: Up to 25 MB.

The Power of Context

One of the most powerful features of this application is the "Context" field. The AI uses this to understand your audio better (e.g., knowing "date a" means "data" in a technical context).

What to write:

  • Subject matter (e.g., "Podcast about machine learning")
  • Speaker names (e.g., "Interview with Dr. Sarah Smith")
  • Technical terms (e.g., "Discussing SQL and NoSQL databases")

3. Reviewing Subtitles

Once processing is complete, you will enter the Review Interface. This is your command center for polishing your subtitles.

The interface is divided into three main areas:

  1. Anomaly Review (Left): Highlights specific issues detected by the AI.
  2. Media Player (Center): Plays your audio/video in sync with the text.
  3. SRT Preview (Right): The full list of subtitle segments.

4. Using the Media Player

The built-in media player allows you to watch or listen to your file while you review.

  • Deep Sync: Clicking any subtitle segment will jump the audio to exactly that point.
  • Auto-Scroll: As the audio plays, the active segment is highlighted.
  • Auto-Pause: The player pauses automatically when you start editing.

5. Fixing Issues & Anomalies

The AI scans your text for potential errors like grammar mistakes or homophones.

  1. Review the flagged text and the AI's explanation.
  2. Use the Suggested Correction to fix it instantly.
  3. Batch Corrections: Fix the same naming or term error across the whole file at once.

6. Manual Editing

You can edit any segment at any time. Hover over a segment and click the Pencil icon.

7. Exporting

  1. Click the Export SRT button in the sidebar.
  2. Your clean, synced .srt file will download immediately.
